Legal

The Legal unit/Agency Attorney reviews legal matters, conducts interviews, prepares cases and represents the agency at administrative hearings and in court as needed. The Agency Attorney advises the Commissioner and assists the Director of Administration and EEO Officer as required; and serves as the agency MWBE Officer. The Agency Attorney negotiates drafts and reviews contracts, RFPs and other legal agreements and ensures compliance with City guidelines. The Legal unit/Agency Attorney analyzes federal, state and local statutes and regulations and assists in the development of legislation, regulations and policy materials. At all times the Agency Attorney performs duties with a focus on protecting the agency from legal exposure.
