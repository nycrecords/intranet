Grant Unit

Each year, the New York State Archives’ Local Government Records Management Improvement Fund (LGRMIF) awards up to $1 million dollars to New York City mayoral agencies. The grants are intended to help establish or improve records management programs. The Grant Administration Unit (GAU) of the Department of Records and Information Services administers the LGRMIF program for city mayoral agencies.

The Grant Administration Unit provides guidance to the agencies in the development of grant projects from the first conceptual stages, to the preparation of final application drafts. The unit submits agencies’ proposals to the State and monitors projects progress for those that receive funding. At the conclusion of grant projects, the Grant Administration Unit prepares comprehensive reports for the State Archives and the New York City Comptroller’s office.
