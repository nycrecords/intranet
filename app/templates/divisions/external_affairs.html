External Affairs

The Community and External Affairs Unit coordinates the public-facing elements of the agency, including its websites, social media, public programs, education, community outreach and marketing. The Unit advances the Department of Records and Information Services’ agenda by developing external relationships, managing strategic implementation of projects and building community support. It is responsible for attracting, welcoming, and retaining visitors to DORIS’ public-facing forums both online and on premises and for managing staff engagement. Using outreach to the media, the public, the city's many communities, and government offices and officials at all levels, the unit endeavors to reach ever-expanding audiences.
