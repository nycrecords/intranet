Executive

The Executive Office consists of the Communications, External Affairs, Finance/Procurement, Human Resources, Information Technology, Legal and Operations Management units and the Commissioner’s Office. Collectively, the staff ensures that all of the agency’s operations run smoothly and that we advance DORIS’s mission of preserving and providing access to the historical and contemporary records of New York City government, ensuring that City records are properly maintained following professional archival and record management practices and to making materials available to diverse communities both online and in person. Major functions include planning for the agency’s future growth and needs, overseeing compliance with City policies and directives, directing efforts to increase awareness of the agency, writing grants, ensuring smooth daily operations and conditions, developing and implementing policy and administering resources for the Municipal Archives, Library and Records Management divisions.
