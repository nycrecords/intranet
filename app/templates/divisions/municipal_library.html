Municipal Library

The Municipal Library is mandated by the New York City Charter as the official repository for New York City agency publications. With access through our print collection and electronic government documents portal, the Library provides a unique set of resources on the history of New York City. Library staff assist researchers with reference requests, catalog print and electronic materials and reach out to city agencies to ensure that they comply with the Charter mandate. Increasingly the Library will be digitizing the collection to expand our user base. Also, of interest to DORIS employees, we maintain a collection of civil service examinations and study guides for various job titles.
