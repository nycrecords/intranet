Operations

The Operations Unit/Operations Manager is in charge of making sure that DORIS staff have safe and comfortable working environments. These means being the liaison to DCAS and Industry City facilities personnel, reporting issues, and responding to building emergencies. The Operations Manager is part of planning new infrastructure improvements and ongoing maintenance. Custodial staff perform daily cleaning and routine maintenance.
