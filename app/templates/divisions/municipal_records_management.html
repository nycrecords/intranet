Municipal Records Management

The Municipal Records Management Division (MRMD) at DORIS serves 2 primary functions:

  • We develop and set citywide policies for records and information management and monitor compliance.
  • We operate storage facilities that hold 1 million cubic feet of active records for New York City agencies.

In order to accomplish these functions, the MRMD team performs the following activities:

  • We develop guidance and document best practices with regards to information and records governance.
  • We assist agencies in creating and maintaining retention schedules.
  • We establish protocols for offices and agencies to follow regarding electronic records management.
  • We work directly with agencies to develop and implement project plans to improve records management practices.
  • We coordinate the transfer, storage, retrieval, and disposal of hard copy records between DORIS and municipal agencies.
